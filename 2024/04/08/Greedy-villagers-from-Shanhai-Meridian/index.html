Greedy villagers from Shanhai Meridian

2024/4/8 Model

Regarding the adaptation of traditional Chinese myths, the content is beginner Maya.

##1. Story background

Storytelling：Greedy villagers

The story comes from a little boy, from a remote and poor village. Because he was sick, his family had no money to ask the doctor to help come over to cure the disease.

One day, a witch doctor came to this village. There is a way that there is a god beast in the place next to the village (on the mountain). Her name is Lu Shu. Her hair can make people healthy and make people a thousand miles a day. As long as she gets her hair, she can solve the village.

The villagers believed the words of the witch doctor. Every day, she wanted to do my best to get her hair with a weapon to live in Lushu. Finally, Lu Shu rushed to be irritable. She was angry. She threw the villagers to the cliffs one by one. Inside, and warned the villagers with her voice, don’t bother her again.
